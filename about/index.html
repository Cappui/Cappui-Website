ABOUT US

Cappui was founded in 2015 by Duncan Forbes, with a mission to creating a more transparent financial and business landscape, reducing fraud and building effective infrastrucutre technologies. Our broad interests are in financial technology, asset transactions, decentralised applications, the blockchain and information management.

Today the world is strife with fraud and regulators are pushing companies to become more open. There is required need for infrastructre and platforms that enable businesses to be open to their clients and customers. Whilst at the same time helping to increase their reputation and build trust.

Cappui builds tools to help in solving these problems, with a commitment to supporting next generation technologies such as CoreOS, IaaS, Ethereum and Virtual Machines.

Cappui is committed to creating products that have global commercial viability. With all the core technologies we produce being designed to help companies worldwide harness them.

Much of the future technoliges we build will be developed openly and be open source along with our venture Famoi.
