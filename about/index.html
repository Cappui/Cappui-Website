ABOUT US

Cappui was founded in 2015 by Duncan Forbes, with a mission to creating a more transparent financial and business landscape, reducing fraud and building effective infrastructure technologies. Our broad interests are in financial technology, asset transactions, decentralised applications, the blockchain, data centers and information management.

The world today sees fraud costing businesses and indviduals billions each year and regulators are pushing companies to become more open and to use secure technologies. The infrastructure and platforms that try to solve these problems currently still do not effectively deliver and are usually costly. We are therefore writing software to tackle this and take you safely from now to tomorrow and continuously into the future.

Cappui builds tools to help in solving these problems, with a commitment to supporting next generation technologies such as CoreOS, IaaS, Ethereum and Virtual Machines.

Cappui is committed to creating products that have global commercial viability. With all the core technologies we produce being designed to help companies worldwide either protect themselves, reduce costs or build genuine reputations.

Much of the future technologies we build will be developed openly and be open source along with our venture Famoi. This is to make sure our products are built with quality in mind.
