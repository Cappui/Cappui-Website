with a love for distruptive technlogies. That looks to build commercially viable ventures focused on financial technology (FinTech) and decentralised applications. Plus various other small micro-projects related to our own personal interests in Internet-of-Things to Art.

What are disruptive technologies?

Disruptive technolgies have the ability to change industries, save time, reduce costs and more importantly solve problems. That focus on infrastructure efficieny and transparency that are vital to thinking ahead to a modern financial and business landscape.

What are ventures?

Ventures are projects we see as globally and commercially viable. We concentrate on a limited number of ventures as they rely on our full attention and time to nurture, scale and grow them as a business that make a return throughout the long-term.

What are Micro-Projects?

Micro-Projects are small experiments that usually focus on the collection of data related to things that we are passionate about and relate to personal interests. A recent example is a list of art fairs that had been collected and reguarly updated over the last year.

VENTURES


We're building the modern day infrastructure for fiduciary asset management. Protecting indviduals, businesses and goverments from fraud, counterfeiting and reducing counterparty risk by automating asset transactions with self-executing smart contracts that no one controls and everyone may trust.
