An Experimental Technology Lab

With a love for disruptive technologies. We want to make both the lives of indviduals and professionals easier. From making banking and asset transactions both cheaper and more efficient; to being able to live in a more transparent world where you have control of your information in a decentralised manner. Our primary focus is Financial Technology (FinTech) and the Blockchain. As well as playing with information that relate to our personal passions and interests. And finding ways to make it useful for others.

Why disruptive technologies?

Disruptive technolgies have the ability to change industries, save time, reduce costs and more importantly solve problems that focus on infrastructure efficieny and transparency that are vital to thinking ahead to a modern financial world.

What are ventures?

Ventures are projects we see as globally and commercially viable. We concentrate on a limited number of ventures as they rely on our full attention and time to nurture, scale and grow them as a business that make a return throughout the long-term.

What are Micro-Projects?

Micro-Projects are small experiments that usually focus on the collection of data related to things that we are passionate about and relate to personal interests. A recent example is a list of art fairs that had been collected and reguarly updated over the last year.

Project .B

Project .B is a codename for our search engine for funds and platforms (Fund Supermarkets/Wrap Platforms). This is a research tool for compliant due diligence and risk management targeted at Financial Advisors and Wealth Managers. We want to create a no fuss tool. That make it easy for users find the information they need and fast. Whilst also leverage the ability to communicate with the funds and platforms. Creating a lead generation tool for these key providers.
